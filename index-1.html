how we work

For each mini mission/goal, we plan to use below proposal/or with minor modifications to be inline with banking system.
  • Set the minimal budget.
  • Let aside initial amount 1-2 lakhs
  • SOme of them for fixed deposit.
  • Some of them in fixed deposit with early dividend opn.
  • Annually withdraw Interest/dividend
  • Refund the mini goal
  • Every 5 or 10 years, combine total
  • Iterate the above process
